About Us - EG Convert

Free online tool to convert between all image formats with just a few clicks

Our Story

EG Convert was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide a free, easy-to-use image conversion tool that works directly in your browser. Our team of developers and designers saw the need for a tool that respects user privacy while delivering high-quality conversions.

Our Mission

We believe that converting images between formats should be simple, secure, and accessible to everyone. That's why we've built a tool that processes your images locally in your browser—your files never leave your computer.

Our Team

We're a small but dedicated team of web developers, UX designers, and digital imaging experts passionate about creating tools that make digital life easier.

Why Choose Us?

  • Privacy-First: Your images are never uploaded to our servers
  • No Registration: Use our tool immediately without creating an account
  • Comprehensive Format Support: Convert between all major image formats
  • Constant Improvement: We regularly update our tool with new features and formats
